# Chapter 10: Additional Professions (Quick Reference)

The following professions follow the same 6-level structure. They are presented in condensed format.

## Painter / Artist

**Primary:** Dexterity | **Secondary:** Charisma | **Related:** Tailor, Teacher

- **Lv1:** Create warning signs, maps, morale art.
- **Lv2:** Create camouflage patterns. Forge documents. Paint signals visible from distance.
- **Lv3:** Create propaganda for settlement morale. Detailed cartography with illustration. Tattoo for identification.
- **Lv4:** Perfect forgery of any document. Trompe-l'oeil (fool-the-eye) camouflage on structures.
- **Lv5:** Create masterwork morale art (settlement +1 saves). Perfect counterfeiting of any visual.
- **Lv6 Hack — Thermochromic Paint:** Mix specific chemicals for paint that changes color with temperature = zombie early-warning on walls (body heat detection).

\page

## Mason / Bricklayer

**Primary:** Strength | **Secondary:** Intelligence | **Related:** Engineer, Miner

- **Lv1:** Basic mortar mixing. Lay bricks in straight lines. Repair cracked walls.
- **Lv2:** Build brick walls and structures. Create fireplaces and chimneys. Repair foundations.
- **Lv3:** Build multi-story brick structures. Create kilns and ovens. Stone cutting.
- **Lv4:** Build defensive fortifications from brick/stone (AC 18). Create underground structures.
- **Lv5:** Fortress-level stonework. Any masonry at advantage. Roman concrete (self-healing material).
- **Lv6 Hack — Roman Concrete Recipe:** Volcanic ash + seawater + lime = concrete that strengthens over centuries and self-heals cracks.

## Glazier / Glass Worker

**Primary:** Dexterity | **Secondary:** Intelligence | **Related:** Engineer, Chemist

- **Lv1:** Cut and install glass panes. Repair broken windows. Safety glass handling.
- **Lv2:** Create bottles and containers. Install windows for weather-sealing. Greenhouse glass work.
- **Lv3:** Blown glass items. Create laboratory glassware. Mirrors from glass + silver.
- **Lv4:** Create lenses (telescopes, microscopes). Fiberglass from glass fiber. Tempered glass.
- **Lv5:** Create any glass item. Bulletproof laminated glass layers. All glasswork at advantage.
- **Lv6 Hack — Glass Knife:** Knapped glass edge is sharper than steel surgical scalpels. (1d6 slashing, advantage on surgery checks)

## Librarian / Archivist

**Primary:** Intelligence | **Secondary:** Wisdom | **Related:** Teacher, Any research profession

- **Lv1:** Organize and catalog found books. Identify valuable texts quickly. Preservation basics.
- **Lv2:** Research efficiently — find specific information in 1/2 time. Cross-reference multiple sources. Book repair.
- **Lv3:** Create indexes and knowledge systems. Students studying in your library gain +25% XP. Preserve books against decay.
- **Lv4:** Create reference systems for entire settlements. Identify forgeries. Historical knowledge grants tactical advantage (advantage on History checks for party).
- **Lv5:** Design and maintain community knowledge base. All research at advantage. Grant +50% XP to all students in library.
- **Lv6 Hack — Speed Reading:** Teach method to triple study speed. (Study sessions take 1/3 time for you and your students)

\page

## Plumber (HVAC / Heating)

**Primary:** Intelligence | **Secondary:** Constitution | **Related:** Plumber, Electrician, Engineer

- **Lv1:** Basic heating system understanding. Bleed radiators. Change filters.
- **Lv2:** Repair furnaces and water heaters. Install wood stoves safely. Ventilation systems.
- **Lv3:** Design heating systems for buildings. Rocket stove mass heaters. Solar thermal collectors.
- **Lv4:** District heating from single source. Geothermal heat pumps. Climate control for greenhouses.
- **Lv5:** Complete HVAC for settlements. Any heating/cooling at advantage. Thermal mass design.
- **Lv6 Hack — Rocket Mass Heater:** Burn wood at 1/10th normal consumption with complete combustion, heat stored in thermal mass for 24h+ from 2h fire.

## Rope Maker / Rigger

**Primary:** Dexterity | **Secondary:** Strength | **Related:** Sailor, Carpenter, Engineer

- **Lv1:** Tie basic knots (bowline, cleat, clove hitch). Use rope safely. Understand load limits.
- **Lv2:** Tie all practical knots. Create rope from natural fibers (DC 12). Rigging and pulley systems.
- **Lv3:** Create rope bridges. Climbing systems. Rappelling rigs. Pulley systems multiply strength 4x.
- **Lv4:** Create zip lines. Complex rigging for construction. Rope traps and snares.
- **Lv5:** Build any rope system at advantage. Suspension bridges. Create fibers from any plant.
- **Lv6 Hack — Grass Rope:** Braid green grass into surprisingly strong cordage. (50lb capacity from literally grass, infinite supply)

## Cobbler / Shoemaker

**Primary:** Dexterity | **Secondary:** Constitution | **Related:** Tanner, Tailor

- **Lv1:** Repair shoes — re-sole, re-stitch, waterproof. Understand foot health.
- **Lv2:** Create basic footwear from leather/rubber. Custom insoles. +5ft movement from proper footwear.
- **Lv3:** Create boots for any terrain. Stealth soles (advantage Stealth). Run-flat soles.
- **Lv4:** Steel-toe from scrap. Climbing boots. Water wading boots. Custom orthotics.
- **Lv5:** Masterwork boots (+10ft movement, advantage Stealth, any terrain). All cobbling at advantage.
- **Lv6 Hack — Tire Sandals:** Cut tire tread into virtually indestructible sandals. (Last decades, any terrain, from waste rubber)

\page

## Chaplain / Spiritual Leader

**Primary:** Wisdom | **Secondary:** Charisma | **Related:** Psychologist, Leader, Teacher

- **Lv1:** Provide comfort to dying/grieving. Perform ceremonies. Offer hope. Remove 1 stress from listeners.
- **Lv2:** Funeral rites prevent morale loss from death. Inspire faith (+1 WIS saves to party). Counsel despair.
- **Lv3:** Bless undertakings (party advantage on one group check/long rest). Maintain community cohesion. Wedding/birth ceremonies boost morale.
- **Lv4:** Inspire fanatical courage (party immune to fear 1 minute/long rest). Build places of worship for settlement morale.
- **Lv5:** Legendary inspiration — party advantage on death saves within 60ft. Community never breaks morale. Spiritual healing (2d6 HP).
- **Lv6 Hack — Last Rites Determination:** Perform rites over dying ally — they gain one final surge (+4 all stats for 1 minute before death, or stabilize on DC 10).

## Barber / Field Surgeon (Historical)

**Primary:** Dexterity | **Secondary:** Constitution | **Related:** Doctor, First Responder

- **Lv1:** Haircuts (morale). Shaving. Basic wound cleaning. Leech application.
- **Lv2:** Tooth pulling. Boil lancing. Minor cauterization. +1 morale from grooming services.
- **Lv3:** Amputation (crude, DC 14). Bloodletting for actual conditions (reducing dangerous blood pressure). Cauterize stumps.
- **Lv4:** Trepanning (relieve brain pressure). Hernia repair. Crude surgery on par with First Responder 4.
- **Lv5:** Barber-surgeon mastery — operate without anesthesia (patient makes CON save vs unconsciousness). All procedures at advantage.
- **Lv6 Hack — Cauterization with Gunpowder:** Pack wound with gunpowder and ignite for instant cauterization (historical practice). (Stops all bleeding instantly, 1d4 fire damage)

\page

## Fitness Instructor / Yoga

**Primary:** Wisdom | **Secondary:** Constitution | **Related:** Athlete, Psychologist, Martial Artist

- **Lv1:** Teach stretching routines. Prevent exercise injuries. Basic meditation.
- **Lv2:** Yoga practice grants advantage on one DEX save/day. Group fitness improves party endurance. Flexibility training.
- **Lv3:** Meditation removes 2 stress/long rest. Breathing exercises grant advantage on CON saves vs exhaustion. Party flexibility training.
- **Lv4:** Advanced meditation — short rest in 15 minutes instead of 1 hour. Teach pain management through breathing.
- **Lv5:** Mind-body mastery — immune to exhaustion from physical activity. Party short rests in 30 minutes. Meditative healing 1d6 HP.
- **Lv6 Hack — Wim Hof Breathing:** Specific hyperventilation technique grants temporary cold immunity and enhanced immune response. (Immune to cold 1 hour, advantage vs disease)

## Beekeeper / Apiarist

**Primary:** Wisdom | **Secondary:** Constitution | **Related:** Farmer, Botanist, Cook

- **Lv1:** Handle bees safely. Harvest honey. Understand hive health. Identify bee-friendly plants.
- **Lv2:** Maintain productive hives. Extract wax for candles/waterproofing. Identify hive diseases.
- **Lv3:** Split hives to expand. Create mead (with Brewer). Wax for wound sealing. Pollinate crops for +50% yields.
- **Lv4:** Breed calmer, more productive bees. Create bee-based perimeter alarm (bees detect vibration). Royal jelly harvest for medicine.
- **Lv5:** Master apiarist — settlement-wide crop pollination. Honey medicine (antiseptic, energy). All at advantage.
- **Lv6 Hack — Propolis Antibiotic:** Bee propolis as natural broad-spectrum antibiotic and antifungal. (Advantage infection saves, treats wounds, from hive)

\page

## Soap Maker / Chandler

**Primary:** Intelligence | **Secondary:** Dexterity | **Related:** Chemist, Cook, Tanner

- **Lv1:** Make basic soap from fat + lye. Create tallow candles. Understand saponification.
- **Lv2:** Make quality soap with scent/properties. Beeswax candles (longer burn). Waterproof wax coatings.
- **Lv3:** Industrial soap production. Specialty soaps (antiseptic, insecticidal). Oil lamps. Lanterns.
- **Lv4:** Create glycerin (explosives precursor with Chemist). Specialized candles (long-burn, smoke-free). Wax seals and preservation.
- **Lv5:** Settlement hygiene program (prevents disease outbreaks). Trade-quality soaps. All at advantage.
- **Lv6 Hack — Fat Lamp from Anything:** Any animal fat in a shell/tin with twisted fabric wick = hours of light. (Infinite light source from scraps)

## Potter / Ceramicist

**Primary:** Dexterity | **Secondary:** Intelligence | **Related:** Mason, Farmer, Cook

- **Lv1:** Shape clay by hand. Air-dry basic containers. Identify clay deposits.
- **Lv2:** Coil and slab construction. Kiln firing (DC 12). Water storage vessels.
- **Lv3:** Wheel throwing (if found). Glazing for waterproofing. Large storage urns. Water filters.
- **Lv4:** Refractory ceramics (withstands extreme heat). Ceramic knives. Pipes and plumbing fixtures.
- **Lv5:** Industrial ceramics. Armor-grade ceramics (AC equivalent to steel, lightweight). All at advantage.
- **Lv6 Hack — Terracotta Evaporative Cooler:** Pot-in-pot with wet sand between = food refrigeration without electricity. (Preserves food 10x longer in hot climates)

# Appendix E: DM Guidelines

## Balancing Professions

- **No profession should replace another entirely.** Each has unique value.
- **Study materials should be rewards**, not guaranteed — make finding a surgery textbook feel like finding a magic item.
- **Level 6 should be RARE.** Most campaigns might see one or two specialists.
- **Life Hacks are one-per-situation** — they're tricks, not infinite resources.

## Pacing Recommendations

| Campaign Phase | Expected Profession Levels |
|:--|:--|
| **Early Survival (Sessions 1-5)** | 1-2 in primary, 0-1 in others |
| **Establishing Base (Sessions 6-15)** | 2-3 in primary, 1-2 in secondaries |
| **Settlement Building (Sessions 16-25)** | 3-4 in primary, 2-3 in secondaries |
| **Late Game (Sessions 26+)** | 4-5 in primary, 3-4 in secondaries |
| **Endgame** | 5-6 in primary (Specialist quest) |

## Stress System (Optional)

Track party stress. Events add stress, rest and activities remove it:

| Event | Stress Added |
|:--|:--|
| Ally death | +3 |
| Near-death | +2 |
| Zombie encounter | +1 |
| Betrayal | +3 |
| Base attacked | +2 |
| Hunger/thirst | +1/day |

| Relief | Stress Removed |
|:--|:--|
| Hot meal | -1 |
| Entertainment | -1 to -2 |
| Safe rest (no threats) | -1 |
| Counseling (Psychologist) | -1 to -2 |
| Alcohol (one drink) | -1 |
| Achievement/victory | -2 |

**At 10+ stress:** Disadvantage on Wisdom saves, risk of panic or breakdown.
