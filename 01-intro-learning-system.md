# Chapter 1: The Learning System

## How Professions Work

Characters develop **Professions** — practical skill sets learned through study, practice, and survival. Every character starts as **Untrained (Level 0)** in everything and advances by finding and studying learning materials.

### Learning Materials

| Material Type | Examples | Study Time | XP Granted |
|:--|:--|:--|:--|
| **Pamphlets & Leaflets** | First aid brochure, recipe card | 1 hour | 15 XP |
| **Manuals & Handbooks** | Car repair manual, field guide | 4 hours | 40 XP |
| **Textbooks** | University biology text | 8 hours | 75 XP |
| **Technical Journals** | Medical journal, IEEE paper | 6 hours | 60 XP |
| **Instructional Videos** | DVD/VHS (requires power) | 2 hours | 50 XP |
| **Mentorship** | Learning from another survivor | Varies | 25 XP/hour |
| **Hands-On Practice** | Performing the skill in the field | Varies | 10-30 XP |
| **Blueprints & Schematics** | Building plans, circuit diagrams | 3 hours | 50 XP |
| **Military Documents** | Field manuals, tactical guides | 6 hours | 75 XP |
| **Ancient/Folk Knowledge** | Herbalism books, survival folklore | 4 hours | 40 XP |

### Study Rules

- Must have **adequate light** and be in a **safe location**
- **Intelligence check (DC 10)** per session; failure = half XP
- Max **8 hours study per long rest** before Exhaustion
- Materials can be **shared** but degrade with use
- Advanced materials have **prerequisite levels**

### Level 6: The Specialist

Level 6 cannot be reached by study alone. A character must:
1. Reach **Level 5 (Master)** in the profession
2. Accumulate **3000 total XP** in that profession
3. Complete a **Defining Moment** — a DM-designed scenario pushing beyond limits
4. Succeed on a **DC 18 ability check** relevant to the profession

Specialists unlock **Life Hacks** — real-world improvised solutions that seem absurd but actually work.

### Multi-Profession Rules

- XP is earned per-profession, not shared
- Only **study one profession** per session
- **Level 3+** in related profession grants **+2** to complementary study checks

### Starting Profession (Optional)

At character creation, players may select **one Primary Profession at Level 2** and **one Secondary Profession at Level 1** to represent their pre-apocalypse career. This is optional — some players may prefer to start fresh.
