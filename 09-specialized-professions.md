# Paramedic / Flight Medic
 

{{wide
*Advanced pre-hospital care, helicopter/vehicle extraction, trauma in transit.*
}}
 
# Primary Ability;

##### Wisdom

# Secondary; 
##### Dexterity  

# Related: 
##### First Responder, Doctor, Combat Medic 

**Prerequisite:** First Responder Level 3

{{classTable,wide
### Paramedic Progression
| Level | Title | Abilities |
|:--:|:--|:--|
| 1 | **Novice** | Intubation and advanced airway (DC 13). IV access in moving vehicle. 12-lead ECG reading (if equipment found). |
| 2 | **Apprentice** | Cardiac arrest management (DC 12). Administer all pre-hospital drugs. Needle decompression. Rapid sequence assessment. |
| 3 | **Journeyman** | Field amputation (DC 13). IO access (bone injection). Manage multiple traumas simultaneously. Coordinate evacuation. |
| 4 | **Expert** | Surgical airway. RSI (sedation for intubation). Mass casualty incident command. Treat chemical/radiation exposure. |
| 5 | **Master** | Any pre-hospital procedure at advantage. Perform surgeon-level interventions in field. Zero-equipment stabilization. |
| 6 | **Specialist** | Unlocks Life Hacks. |
}}
{{classTable,wide
### Level 6 Hacks
| Title | Description |  |
|:----------|:--------------------------:|:-----------------:|
|**Finger Thoracostomy:**| Finger through chest wall to release tension pneumothorax without tubes. <br> (Saves life with zero equipment) |
|**Towel Tourniquet:**| Rolled towel + stick as improvised tourniquet when no CAT available. <br> (Stops arterial bleeding) |
|**Rectal Fluid Administration:**| Administer fluids rectally when IV access impossible (proctoclysis). <br> (Rehydrates unconscious patients) |
}}
