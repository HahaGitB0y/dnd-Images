# Marksman / Sharpshooter
 

{{wide
*Firearms proficiency, long-range accuracy, weapon maintenance.*
}}
 
# Primary Ability;

##### Dexterity

# Secondary; 
##### Wisdom  

# Related: 
##### Hunter, Military Tactician, Gunsmith 


{{classTable,wide
### Marksman Progression
| Level | Title | Abilities |
|:--:|:--|:--|
| 1 | **Novice** | Safely handle firearms. Load/unload. Basic stance. Shoot at short range (no penalty up to 30ft). |
| 2 | **Apprentice** | Proficient with handguns and rifles. No penalty up to normal range. Basic weapon cleaning. Identify ammo types. |
| 3 | **Journeyman** | Advantage on aimed shots (using full action). Shoot at long range without disadvantage. Quick reload (bonus action). ID bullet caliber by sound. |
| 4 | **Expert** | Called shots — target limbs, head at -2 instead of -5. Shoot through cover (ignore half cover). Maintain weapons to prevent jamming. |
| 5 | **Master** | All ranged attacks at advantage within normal range. Called shots at no penalty. Headshot instant-kills standard zombies (DC 13 DEX). |
| 6 | **Specialist** | Unlocks Life Hacks. |
}}
{{classTable,wide
### Level 6 Hacks
| Title | Description |  |
|:----------|:--------------------------:|:-----------------:|
|**Subsonic Ammo Craft:**| Reduce powder loads for near-silent shots that don't attract hordes. <br> (-1 damage but no sound beyond 50ft) |
|**Improvised Suppressor:**| Oil filter or plastic bottle as single-use sound suppressor. <br> (Reduces noise by 75%, 1d4 uses) |
|**Ported Barrel Compensation:**| File precise ports in barrel for zero-recoil follow-up shots. <br> (+2 to hit on second shot same turn) |
|**Wax Slug Shotgun:**| Fill shotgun shell with melted wax = single-projectile slug from birdshot. <br> (Doubles range, +2 damage) |
